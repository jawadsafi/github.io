About Us!

We are ITS

Welcome to ITS Computer Shop, your go-to destination for all things tech! Since our founding in 2005, we've been dedicated to providing top-quality products and exceptional customer service to computer users throughout the community. Our mission is simple: to help people make the most of their technology. Whether you're a seasoned pro or just starting out, our team of experienced professionals is here to help you find the right solutions to meet your needs. We believe that technology should be accessible to everyone, and we're committed to making it easy and affordable to get the latest and greatest gear. At ITS Computer Shop, we offer a wide range of products and services, including custom-built desktops, laptops, tablets, and accessories from all the leading brands. We also offer repair and maintenance services for all types of devices, from software troubleshooting to hardware upgrades and replacements. Our team is made up of certified experts with years of experience in the field. We're passionate about technology, and we love nothing more than helping our customers find the perfect solutions to meet their needs. Whether you're a gamer, a professional, or just looking for a reliable computer for your home or office, we're here to help.


We take pride in our commitment to customer service, and we strive to make every interaction with our customers a positive one. We believe that building relationships with our customers is the key to our success, and we're always here to answer questions, provide advice, and offer support whenever you need it. In addition to our commitment to our customers, we're also passionate about giving back to our community. We're proud to support local charities and initiatives that help make our community a better place to live and work. We're honored to have received several awards and accolades over the years, including the "Best Computer Shop" award from the local Chamber of Commerce. But for us, the greatest reward is the satisfaction of knowing that we've helped our customers get the most out of their technology. Thank you for considering ITS Computer Shop for all your technology needs. We look forward to serving you!
